About us



Who are we

The Centre for Health Services Research is the client of this project. Enhancing the effectiveness of health service delivery is one of their ambitions. One of the main subjects the centre are concentrating on is Telehealth, which is new technology to help healthcare providers deliver their service online.



What is the website

"High Five Health"is a web application aimed to provide a platform for patients to rate their health services across all healthcare platforms and encourage them to leave feedbacks towards the health services they have experienced. The web application provides a simple user interface and allows users to rate the health services in five aspects effectiveness, friendliness, cost-benefit, convenience and health benefit of the service. Once an effective rating made, the user would be rewarded by virtual encouragement.
